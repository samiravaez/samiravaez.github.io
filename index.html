About Me

I obtained my MSc in Computer Science from K. N. Toosi University of Technology, where I was a part of the Textual Intelligent Systems Research Group. Under the supervision of Dr. Saeed Farzi, I worked on bias and unfairness in recommender systems during my graduate studies. Before that, I received my bachelor's degree from Shahid Beheshti University (SBU). My research interests center around recommender systems as well as bias and fairness in information retrieval.
